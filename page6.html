SEGREGATION

Continue the advocacy, bring back our world from being healthy, segregate properly.

  

  

THESE TWO IMAGES REPRESENTS THE CAUSE OF THE CURRENT BIGGEST PROBLEM THAT WE ARE FACING. 
DISPOSABLE MATERIALS

SINCE WE ARE RELYING ON THESE PRODUCTS, WE TEND TO CONSUME AS MUCH AS POSSIBLE, LEADING TO MORE WASTES THAT WE ARE PUTTING AWAY THUS CONTRIBUTING TO THE POLLUTION.

how can we atleast reduce the problem?

follow these 3 simple steps!

  1. INVEST TO ALMOST EVERLASTING THINGS - with this, we can avoid the land pollution by less consumption of disposable materials and buying utensils or forks made out of metals and ceramic.
  2. DON'T BUY UNLESS YOU NEED IT! - many people are buying things for future use like fiestas, birthdays and such. But if you are going to buy disposable materials too early, it might be too outdated and not suitable for its purpose thus it will end up to the garbage can.
  3. LESS PACKAGES, THE BETTER - this step is very efficient not only for our environment but also to our consumers, the less package/wrap, the less plastic we will use, and the less plastic we will use in our packaging, the more realistic the product will it be because at this point in time, we are very used to the companies selling their products in a very large packaging, but with a very small content.
