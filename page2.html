Waste materials can be used to create miniatures of vehicles to be used as decoration for your house.

Materials: You can use softfrink cans for the body, popsickle sticks out of ice creams for the 
support, bottle caps for head lights and finally, for the wheels you can use the bottom part of plastic water bottles enwrapped with used illustration boards. 

Adhesives: It is better to use glue sticks because it will stick harder than the usual glue.

Large plastic bottles can be used for lamp covers.

Materials: 1 Bottle of any empty 1.5 liter plastic bottle and paint for design

Note: It is very helpful for the people who likes dim light for reading purposes.

Plastic bottles can be used as foam replacer to the grilled chairs.

Materials: Plastic Bottles (many enough to cover the back part and the seat part) and straw ropes to bind them in the grills. You can also use glue sticks for additional stability.

Note: Be sure to cut the edges of the bottles so that you will not feel any discomfort.

Plastic spoon and forks can be used to make flowerly decorations!

Materials: plastic spoon/fork, glue stick and can lid for center piece

Note: If made carefully, it will be an aesthetic decoration especially in the living room.
